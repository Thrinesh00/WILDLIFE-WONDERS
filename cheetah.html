About the Cheetah

The Cheetah (Acinonyx jubatus) is the fastest land mammal and can move at a speed of up to 103 km per hour over a distance of hundreds of meters.

Scientific Name:

Acinonyx Jubatus

Habitat:

grasslands, savannahs, shrublands, dense vegetation, mountainous terrain, and temperate to hot deserts.
