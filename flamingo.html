About the Flamingo

Flamingos or flamingoes are a type of wading bird in the family Phoenicopteridae, which is the only extant family in the order Phoenicopteriformes.
There are four flamingo species distributed throughout the Americas, and two species native to Afro-Eurasia.
A group of flamingoes is called a "flamboyance". Wikipedia
