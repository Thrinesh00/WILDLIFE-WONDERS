About the Peacock

Peafowl is a common name for two bird species of the genus Pavo and one species of the closely related genus Afropavo within the tribe Pavonini of the family Phasianidae.
Male peafowl are referred to as peacocks, and female peafowl are referred to as peahens. Wikipedia

Santific name :

Pavo cristatus.

Lifespan:

Indian peafowl: 15 years

Mass:Indian peafowl:

4 – 6 kg,

Green peafowl:

3.8 – 5 kg

Term for young:

peachick

Habitat:

open lowland forests, as well as farms and agricultural fields.
