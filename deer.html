About the Deer:

Antlers

Most male deer and some female deer have antlers, which are made of bone and grow each spring and summer. Deer shed their antlers after the breeding season in late winter.

Habitat

Deer are native to Europe, Asia, North America, South America, and northern Africa. They have been introduced to many other places as game animals.

Diet

Deer eat grass, twigs, bark, and shoots.

Hunting

Deer are hunted for their meat, hides, and antlers.

Scientific Name:

Cervidae

Habitat:

forests, deserts, tundra, swamps, and high mountainsides.
